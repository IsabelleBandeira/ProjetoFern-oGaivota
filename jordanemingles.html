    Biography

    Many name Michael Jordan as ‘the living legend of basketball’. And they say that rightly in the world, the player changed the history of the sport in his country and around the world.

    It turns out that the NBA monster was not always valued. While still in school, Jordan was simply cut off from the high school basketball team, which made him lock himself in his room and cry for many hours. Still, he persisted with determination and determination. He trained a lot before and after achieving success and was known for being the first to arrive and the last to leave training. So much effort has led Michael to be elected best player countless times and to inspire people, athletes or not.p>
