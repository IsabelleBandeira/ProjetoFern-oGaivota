  • She who sings wards off all worries away

    Taylor Swift

    She was only 11 at the time, but the young and ambitious Taylor Swift had to struggle at first to find a record company in Nashville, Tennessee, to sign a contract with her.

    During spring break from high school, she made a demo CD of her karaoke covers from country stars like Dolly Parton, Dixie Chicks and LeAnn Rimes for Music Row and handed copies to as many record label receptionists as she could, but she said that nobody wanted it because "everyone in that city wanted to do what I wanted to do." She clearly found her niche, although this musical princess has since learned to play various instruments, has taken over the country's country and pop-rock scene and has openly challenged one of the most famous streaming music companies.
